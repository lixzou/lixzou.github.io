Lixin Zou

Associate Professor of Wuhan University

Lixin Zou (邹立新) is an Associate Professor at the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Wuhan University. Before that, he was a Staff Algorithm Engineer at Baidu Search Science team from Jul. 2020 to Nov. 2022. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science & Technology from Tsinghua University in 2020. Currently, he focuses on efficient large language model and its application, web search and recommender system.


Note: I am looking for self-motivated students to do long-term research with me! If you are interested in working with me, please drop me an email.
